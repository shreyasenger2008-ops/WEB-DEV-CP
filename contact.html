Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tour?

Visit our Book Now page, select your preferred destination and experience, fill in your details, and complete the payment process.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel your booking up to 7 days before the tour date for a full refund. Cancellations made within 7 days will incur a 25% cancellation fee.

Are meals included in the packages?

Meal inclusions depend on the package you choose. You can select from breakfast only, half-board, or full-board options during booking.

What is your commitment to sustainability?

We are 100% carbon neutral and committed to preserving Maharashtra's ecosystems. A portion of every booking goes to local environmental conservation projects.

Do you provide transportation?

Yes, all our packages include transportation to and from the destination. Pickup points are available in major cities in Maharashtra.

Can I customize my package?

Absolutely! Contact us with your preferences and we'll create a custom itinerary tailored to your needs and interests.
