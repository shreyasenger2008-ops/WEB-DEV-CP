About EcoTourism Maharashtra

Our mission to promote sustainable tourism and cultural preservation

Our Story

EcoTourism Maharashtra was founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a platform that enables travelers to experience the beauty, culture, and nature of Maharashtra while supporting local communities and protecting the environment.

We believe that tourism should benefit everyone – travelers get authentic experiences, local communities get economic opportunities, and nature gets protection and conservation efforts. This is the foundation of our platform.

Today, we've facilitated over 10,000 eco-tourism experiences across more than 50 destinations in Maharashtra, creating employment for local guides, artisans, and hospitality workers while maintaining 100% carbon neutrality.

Our Core Values

What drives everything we do

Sustainability

We minimize environmental impact through carbon-neutral operations and support conservation efforts in every destination.

Community First

Local communities are at the heart of our operations. We ensure fair wages and support local economic development.

Authenticity

We offer genuine, immersive experiences that celebrate Maharashtra's rich culture and heritage.

Education

We educate travelers about environmental conservation and cultural significance of each destination.
